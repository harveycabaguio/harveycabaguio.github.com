About

Stranded is the story of an astronaut. An astronaut on an alien planet littered with dusty canyons, mysterious ruins, and hulking steel giants. The astronaut has but six Earth-days of oxygen left, leaving a short time to explore the planet and interact with its peaceful inhabitants. There's an intriguing dichotomy between the beauty of the planet�including the majesty of the steel giants�and the loneliness and impending death of the astronaut.

This story will be experienced as a downloadable point and click adventure game for elementary OS, Linux, Mac OS X, and Windows.

Stranded is planned to be available for download in early 2014. Follow Peter Moorhead on Twitter for updates.

Development

Stranded is being developed by Peter Moorhead in Python on elementary OS. The background art is beautifully crafted by Lucas "Midio" Carvalho, the character art is lovingly pixel-perfected by Glauber Kotaki, and the serene soundtrack and eerie audio are from the mind of Joe "Stux" Edwards.

This website was designed and developed by Cassidy James, Daniel For�, and other members of the elementary community.

Stranded has been under development for over a year.
