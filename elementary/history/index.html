elementary's History

Learn how elementary became so big

2007

elementary Icons

elementary started out as an iconset.

2008

GTK Theme

After designing the iconset, the next step was to design a user interface that complemented the icons.

2010

Nautilus Elementary

Stemming from the Ubuntu 100 papercuts project, Nautilus Elementary could be considered the first true elementary app.

Late 2010

More Apps

Towards the end of the year elementary grew even more than anticipated - two new apps made their first release. Dexter, a contacts manager and Postler, an email client.

2011

elementary OS "Jupiter"

Named after the king of the Roman gods, Jupiter is the combination of elementary's previous efforts into one lightweight OS.

2013

elementary OS "Luna"

Effectively starting over from scratch, elementary OS "Luna" boasts a whole new desktop environment accompanied by a bigger suite of apps, all sharing the fabulous design elementary boasts.
